My research focuses on Human-AI interaction, a natural progression of my prior background in Human-Computer Interaction. Specifically, I concentrate on combining the respective strengths of computer vision and human intelligence to classify and organize large image collections. This work has been published at HCOMP 2023, and won the best demo award at the same conference. Additionally, I achieved a Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics to better understand methods in processing and presenting big data.
